About Me

I am currently take a role of Expert Researcher at Tencent Ads. I enjoy working in Deep Learning, Natural Language Understanding, Information Retrieval. Prior to joining Tencent in 2021, I worked as a senior staff engineer/Research Scientist at Ant Financial. Before that, I was a senior staff engineer at Baidu from April 2014 to December 2017. I also worked in Taobao Recommendation System Group before joining Baidu from 2012 to 2014. In Ant Financial, I led the Ant Group NLP framework and services from 0 to 1. These services integrated self-developed pre-trained model as the backbone. Also provided other 10+ services, including lexical/syntactic analyzer, information extraction, question answering, etc. Before Ant Financial, I introduced deep learning technology into Baidu Search, Ads, Map with PaddlePaddle v0.10.5. I led/joined projects: Query Rewriting, Traffic condition forecasting, CTR prediction for Ads, etc. I have 10+ publications at important NLP conference and 10+ pending and approved patents. These methods commonly userd in industry. And some of them have achieved the first place or state of the art results on important leaderboard or evaluation tasks.
